Headspace


 

Headspace is an app that offers guided meditation and relaxation techniques. It helps users work on their restlessness, sleep, anxiety, stress, and more. Meditation is a very effective way to gain more self-discipline and de-stress. I recently started using headspace and I already feel more in control and relaxed. The original price is $69.99 a year, but with the student discount, it's $9.99.
https://www.headspace.com/headspace-meditation-app

Waking Up by Sam Harris


     

Waking Up by Sam Harris is a meditation app that offers a series of guided meditation taught by the Neuroscientist and New York Times Best Seller, Sam Harris. I personally have not used this app yet, but it has helped people in my family handle anxiety and stress. Although the price is originally $99.99, users who cannot afford it have have the option of using it for free.
https://www.wakingup.com/

Breathe by Dr. Jud


Breathe by Dr.Jud is an app meant to help people manage their stress and anxiety through simple breathing exercises. Dr.Jud is a researcher at MIT, Yale, and Brown who studies negative thinking and behavior patterns. He has written various articles focused on mental health and is the founder of anxiety,smoking, and eating programs. 'Breathe' is completely free for Apple and Android users!
https://apps.apple.com/us/app/breathe-by-dr-jud/id1472340683(for Apple)
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.claritasmindsciences.Breathe&hl=en_US(for Android)

Happify


Happify uses activities, games, and meditation to lower stress levels and better mood. Although the app offers in-app purchases, it offers various free tracks that students can use. These tracks teach meditation, managing stress in uncertain times, conquering negative thoughts, and more.
https://www.happify.com/

MoodMission


MoodMission is an app that teaches users strategies to cope with anxiety and depression. Users are given 5 "missions" to help them improve their mental health. Each mission is derived from credible scientific research and effectively explains how it is helping you. The app is offered for free and in-app purchases are $4.99.
http://moodmission.com/
